Client's Goals:

- To provide a modern, intuitive, and engaging communication/collaboration platform for its growing staff.
- To provide a way to automate back-office processes
- To provide a way to quickly spin up teams for project work

Solution:

- Built a modern, beautiful collaboration solution on top of SharePoint Online including social features to promote employee engagement
- Numerous customizations which further facilitate collaboration and address the client's specific needs
- An intuitive Project Portal which automates the process of spinning up project teams and providing them with a digital workplace to collaborate

Outcome:

- Client feels they have significantly increased their ROI from SharePoint Online
- Encouraging users to adopt the new system has proven easier than initially expected
- Measurable productivity increases due to automation of back-office processes and easier document collaboration
